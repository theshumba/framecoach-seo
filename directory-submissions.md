## 2. Product Hunt

### Submission Details
- **Submit URL:** https://www.producthunt.com/posts/new (requires account)
- **Launch page:** Set up via https://www.producthunt.com/ — click "Submit" or "Launch" button
- **Cost:** Free
- **Review time:** Launches go live at 12:01 AM PT on chosen launch day
- **Manual or automated:** Manual (requires careful preparation and community engagement)
- **Account needed:** Free Product Hunt account (sign up at producthunt.com)

### What Info Is Needed
- Product name
- Tagline (max 60 characters)
- Website URL
- Description (up to 260 characters for short, longer for description body)
- Topics/tags (select from Product Hunt's taxonomy)
- Thumbnail image (240x240 px)
- Gallery images/GIF (1270x760 px recommended, up to 5 images)
- Maker comment (first comment on your launch)
- Launch video (optional but highly recommended, 1-2 min)
- Hunter (someone to "hunt" your product — can be yourself)

### Pre-Written Submission

**Product Name:** FrameCoach

**Tagline (60 chars max):** Real-time camera coaching for indie filmmakers

**Short Description (260 chars):**
FrameCoach coaches filmmakers through camera settings, composition, and shot choices in real-time on set. Stop guessing ISO, aperture, and framing — start creating with confidence. Free at framecoach.io.

**Full Description:**
Hey Product Hunt! We built FrameCoach because every filmmaker has been there — on set, staring at their camera, unsure which settings to use.

FrameCoach is the decision layer for your camera. It coaches you through:

- **Camera settings** — ISO, aperture, shutter speed, white balance
- **Composition** — rule of thirds, leading lines, framing techniques
- **Shot choices** — when to use which focal length and why
- **Visual storytelling** — how settings affect the mood and look of your footage

Built for indie filmmakers, film students, videographers, and content creators who want to stop memorizing settings and start understanding cinematography.

Free to use at https://framecoach.io

**Topics:** Filmmaking, Photography, Education, Creative Tools, Video Streaming

**Maker Comment (first comment):**
Hey PH! I'm building FrameCoach because I got tired of watching 30-minute tutorials when I just needed a quick answer on set. FrameCoach is like having a cinematography mentor in your pocket — it tells you what settings to use AND teaches you why. Would love your feedback. What camera decisions do you struggle with most?

### Launch Tips
- Launch on a Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday (highest traffic)
- Prepare 5+ gallery images showing the app in action
- Have a 60-second demo video ready
- Reach out to 50+ people to upvote in the first 4 hours (friends, community members)
- Respond to every comment within minutes
- Share on Twitter/X with #producthunt hashtag
- Do NOT ask for upvotes — ask people to "check it out on Product Hunt"

## 8. AppSumo Marketplace

### Submission Details
- **Apply URL:** https://sell.appsumo.com/ (redirects to application form at https://share.hsforms.com/2iUvbadKTQ4GR29FDPAjFWgcbi47)
- **Cost:** Free to apply; zero upfront listing fees. AppSumo takes a revenue share on sales.
- **Acceptance rate:** ~10% (highly selective)
- **Review time:** Varies (weeks to months)
- **Manual or automated:** Manual (application form, then work with partner manager if accepted)
- **Account needed:** Apply via HubSpot form

### What Info Is Needed (Application Form)
- Product name and URL
- Product description
- Current pricing model
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Number of existing customers
- Team size
- Willingness to offer a lifetime deal
- Customer support capacity

### The 4-Step Process
1. **Apply** — Submit product for review. Team evaluates quality, market fit, partner stability.
2. **Prepare** — If accepted (~10%), work with partner manager on deal structure and pricing. Beta-test with real users.
3. **Launch** — Product goes live to 1.5M+ entrepreneurs for a 60-day campaign window. AppSumo provides email, ads, affiliates, and video support.
4. **Scale** — Top performers get additional marketing resources.

### Pre-Written Application Description

**Product Name:** FrameCoach

**Product URL:** https://framecoach.io

**Description:**

It coaches users through ISO, aperture, shutter speed, white balance, and composition in real-time on set. Target market: 15M+ indie filmmakers, 50M+ content creators, and 500K+ film students globally.

**Proposed Deal:** Lifetime access to FrameCoach Pro (if a paid tier exists or is planned)

### Honest Assessment
- AppSumo works best for SaaS products with a paid tier that can offer lifetime deals.
- If FrameCoach is currently 100% free with no paid features, AppSumo is NOT the right fit yet.
- Revisit when/if FrameCoach launches a Pro/Premium tier.
- AppSumo's audience is entrepreneurs and small business owners — not the core filmmaker audience, but there's overlap with content creators.
