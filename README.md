# FrameCoach SEO

SEO content marketing assets and campaign materials for **[FrameCoach](https://framecoach.io)** — a free, real-time camera coaching app for indie filmmakers and content creators. This repository contains the keyword research, content strategy, site audits, and cross-posting automation that drives organic discovery for FrameCoach.

## What Is FrameCoach?

FrameCoach is a free, real-time camera coaching app for indie filmmakers and content creators. It provides on-screen guidance to help you master professional cinematography techniques — from framing and composition to exposure and camera movement. Whether you are shooting your first short film or levelling up your content creation, FrameCoach is your on-set coaching companion.

- **X/Twitter:** [@framecoachapp](https://x.com/framecoachapp)
- **Blog:** [theshumba.github.io/framecoach-blog](https://theshumba.github.io/framecoach-blog/)

## What This Repo Does

This is the SEO operations hub for FrameCoach. It contains everything needed to grow organic traffic and establish FrameCoach as a recognised entity across search engines and AI systems:

### Keyword Research
- 100 filmmaking keywords researched and prioritised by search volume, competition, and relevance
- Keyword clustering by topic (camera settings, composition, gear, storytelling)

### Content Assets
- 50+ SEO-optimised blog post drafts covering filmmaking education topics
- Content calendar with publishing schedule
- Social media templates and platform-specific bios

### Site Audit
- Technical SEO audit of framecoach.io with specific HTML fix recommendations
- GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) audit for AI search visibility
- Structured data and schema markup guidance

### Cross-Posting Automation
- Scripts for syndicating blog content to Medium and Dev.to
- Cron-based scheduling for automated cross-posting
- Platform submission guides for Product Hunt, AlternativeTo, and other directories

### Search Console & Analytics
- Google Search Console setup guide
- Platform and directory submission tracker
