# Social Media Post Templates for Blog Promotion

Use these templates when sharing blog posts. Customize for each post.

---

## Twitter/X Templates

### Educational Hook
```

Here's how to fix it (and why it matters more than your camera): [link]
```

### Stat/Fact Hook
```
Spielberg still shoots at 24fps and 1/48 shutter.

Here's why those numbers matter (and how to use them yourself): [link]

#filmmaking #cinematography
```

### Question Hook
```
Quick — what's more important for cinematic footage: a $2000 camera or a $20 reflector?

(The answer might surprise you): [link]
```

## Reddit Post Templates

### r/Filmmakers Format
```
Title: [I wrote a guide on/I compiled/Here's my breakdown of] [topic]

Body:
Hey everyone — I put together a guide on [topic] based on [experience/research].

[2-3 sentence summary of what the post covers]

Key takeaways:
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]

Full article here if you want the deep dive: [link]

Happy to answer any questions in the comments.
```

### r/cinematography Format (more technical)
```
Title: [Technical topic] — a practical guide

Body:
[Dive straight into the technical content — this sub values substance]

[Include 2-3 practical tips directly in the Reddit post — don't just link-bait]

Wrote this up in more detail with examples here: [link]
```

**Reddit Rules:**
- Post genuine value in the Reddit post itself (not just a link)
- Respond to every comment
- Don't post more than 1-2x per week per subreddit
- Build karma through commenting before posting
- Never mention FrameCoach in Reddit posts — let the blog article do that naturally
