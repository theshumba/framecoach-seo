## Content Strategy

### Hub-and-Spoke Model
- **Hub 1:** "FrameCoach — The Filmmaker App" → Links FROM posts 41-50, 81-85
- **Hub 2:** "Learn Filmmaking — Beginner's Resource Center" → Links FROM posts 31-40
- **Hub 3:** "Camera Settings Guide for Filmmakers" → Links FROM posts 11-20

### Publishing Priority
1. Start with Cluster A (Camera Settings) — closest to FrameCoach's core feature
2. Then Cluster C (Beginner Filmmaking) — highest search volume
3. Then Cluster D (Indie Tools) — most commercial intent
4. Then everything else

### Cross-Linking Rules
- Every post mentions FrameCoach naturally once in intro/outro
- Every post links to framecoach.io at least once
- Every post links to 2-3 other blog posts (internal linking)
- Hub pages link to all their spoke posts
