FrameCoach — The Decision Layer for Your Camera

FrameCoach is a free, real-time camera coaching app for indie filmmakers and content creators.

It guides you through ISO, aperture, shutter speed, white balance, and composition decisions while you're on set — so you can focus on telling your story instead of second-guessing settings.

Key Features

  • Real-time camera settings coaching — Get guided through ISO, aperture, shutter speed, and white balance for any shooting scenario
  • Shot composition guidance — Rule of thirds, leading lines, depth, and framing suggestions as you shoot
  • Scene-aware decisions — Lighting-aware exposure recommendations that adapt to your environment
  • Plain-language explanations — No jargon. Understand why each setting matters for the look you want

Who It's For

Solo filmmakers, indie crews, content creators, and film students who don't have a dedicated camera department. Whether you're shooting your first short film or your hundredth YouTube video, FrameCoach helps you nail the technical decisions so you can focus on the creative ones.

Free to Use

FrameCoach is completely free. No subscription, no paywall, no hidden fees. We believe camera knowledge should be accessible to every filmmaker regardless of budget.
